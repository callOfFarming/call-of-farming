Here are some things you can ask of me:
Say "Stop" and I will stop talking right away.
Say "what can I plant" to get a list of the seeds you have available.
Say "current task" and I will tell you what you are currently doing, presumably asking me dumb questions.
Say "current date" to get the number of days since you've arrived, because you so desperately want to escape.
There are some additional actions you can do with your plots and plants:
Say "Harvest plot 1" to harvest plot 1 if it is ready for harvesting.
Say "Plant potato" and one packet of your potato seeds if you have any will be used on a free plot if you have any.
Say "Sell 4 potatoes" and I will take 4 potatoes from you if you have enough, and give you buckaroos for the produce.
Say "Buy 2 potato seeds" and I will take your money if you have enough, and give you 2 packets of potato seeds.
You can ask about price of things too: Say "Look up seed prices" for a list of which seeds are currently available to buy and their respective prices.
Say "Look up produce prices" for a list of produce currently available to sell and their respective prices.

Try playing this game with the speaker on, and don't look!
