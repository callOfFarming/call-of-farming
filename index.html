Help content
Say "stop" to stop me from talking at any time.
Say "add color" to add a drop of color, (example: "add red", alternatively just say "red")
Say "upgrade color" to upgrade a color so that more of it gets added each time, (example: "Upgrade red").
Say "prestige" to sacrifices all ink levels to increase opacity. This also adds a prestige point.
Say "status" to get an overview of current ink levels.
Say "blindfold on" to hide the ink levels and play by sound only.
Say "blindfold off" to show the ink levels on screen. You can still play by sound only.

Try playing this game with the speaker on, and don't look!
